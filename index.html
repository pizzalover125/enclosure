FAQ

What is Enclosure?

Enclosure is a You Ship, We Ship that helps you design and receive custom cases for your personal items.

How do I get started?

Click the "Get Started" button above and follow the instructions to build your first case!

Who can participate?

Anyone under the age of 19.

How does fulfillment take place?

If you do not have a 3D printer, fulfillment will happen through #printing-legion, a program where Hack Clubbers with a 3D printer ship you a case! Otherwise, you can print it yourself.
