Step 0

Create accounts on Onshape and GrabCAD. You will need them later.

Step 1

On Google, search up "GrabCAD [your phone name]". You should be able to find your phone there. Click on the top link and download relavent files.

Step 2

Create a new document on Onshape and import your case.

Step 3

Draw a sketch on the top layer. Make a rectangle around the phone by pressing r and centering around the middle. Next, press d and create a dimension of 0.25 between all of the edges. Repeat, but with 2mm spacing instead.

Step 4

Press the button that looks like a rounded corner and filet every edge. The radius does not matter. After you select all 8, click off. Add a dimension of 0.25 mm for the smaller rectangle arcs and 2.725 mm for the bigger arcs. Note that we do 2.725 mm to make the corners more resistant to damage.

Step 5

Look up the thickness of your phone and create and extrude that thickness.

Step 6

Extrude the back panel by another 1 mm.

Step 7

Extrude the camera cutout and the flash cutout inwards.

Step 8

Measure the distances between the holes on the bottom and the sides. Create a rectangle with those distances and extrude it inwards.

Step 9

Measure the distances between the side buttons and the edges. Create a rectangle with those distances and extrude it inwards. Do this for the other side as well :)

Step 10

Delete the phone and export the case. Congrats, you're done!
